      Temperature Controlled Transport Solutions

      One of the biggest challenges in development of a resilient cold chain infrastructure in India is the limited supply of good quality temperature controlled vehicles in the country. With our owned fleet of reefer trucks fitted with IoT sensors for active temperature monitoring across the container’ we have the capability to provide state-of-the art vehicles for deployment as per your business needs PAN India.

      We ensure a clean and a controlled environment inside the container so as to maintain product integrity and provide unmatched reliability to our customers with end to end cargo visibility and temperature monitoring.

      Customized Solution

      • Owned fleet with varied capacities (32/24/20 Feet Containers)
      • Container modification
      • Dedicated deployment
      • PAN India Services

      Product Integrity

      • Varied Temperature range (-25 to 25 Degree Celsius)
      • Strip Curtains in the containers to maintain temperature
      • Compliance with cold chain transport regulations
      • Reefer Power Backup

      Cargo Visibility & Temperature Monitoring

      • Temperature sensors across the container
      • Centralized control tower support for end to end cargo visibility
      • Independent offline data loggers inside the container
